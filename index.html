Eng. Eugene Roshal

The Man who made Winrar

Roshal is best known as the developer of Winrar

Here's a time line of Eng. Eugene Roshal:

  • Born in : 10 March 1972 In Russia
  • 1993 : Developed The RAR file format
  • 1995 : Developed WinRAR file archiver
  • 1996 : Developed FAR file manager

Best Of His Acts : The RAR compression algorithm is officially owned by his elder brother Alexander, because Eugene Roshal has "no time to concern himself with software development and copyright-related issues at the same time."
